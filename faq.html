Q: Why should I use this app?
A: Heroes are faster, safer, and more reliable than police and uber drivers. In recent times, their numbers have grown significantly, reaching a scale that makes it finally possible to harness them for mass transportation at an affordable cost.
Q: How do you recruit the heroes? How can I know that my hero is trustworthy?
A: We recruit via LinkedIn! Our vetting process is a rigorous one and involves multiple rounds of mock interviews. Nobody wants a villain!
Q: How do I request a hero?
A: Simply download our app, then tap a button to begin. Your hero will arrive shortly.
Q: How much does it cost?
A: Since Cape Escape is a marketplace for transportation, the price you pay is based on factors such as distance and availability of heroes. You set the maximum price you’re willing to pay for a given hero and then Cape Escape matches you with a hero that’s willing to accept your price.
Q: How does it work?
A: Our product is powered by a complex algorithm which efficiently matches idle heroes with users based on factors such as proximity and shortest time-to-destination. The system is built on a serverless architecture, which makes running and scaling our backend services simple and cost-effective, allowing us to reliably serve the needs of Cape Escape's ever growing user base.
Q: What if I have a complaint about my hero?
A: Cape Escape is a customer obsessed company. We value each customer and want to ensure a positive experience. Therefore, we’ve staffed our customer service team with serverless chatbots that are available 24/7 to assist you.
Q: How do I cancel my hero if I no longer need their help?
A: Tap the “Cancel Hero” button in the Cape Escape app.
Q: Can I use Cape Escape internationally?
A: Yes, you can use Cape Escape in most countries except for Antarctica, Cuba, Sudan, Iran, North Korea, Syria and any other country designated by the United States Treasury's Office of Foreign Assets Control.
Q: How do I pay for my hero?
A: After creating a Cape Escape account, fill in your payment method such as credit card, debit card, Bitcoin wallet, or your choice of ice cream or steak. After you complete your experience, you will automatically be charged the fare.
Q: How many people can my hero assist?
A: The number of people depends on the size of your problem. Most heroes can take one person at a time. You can also request multiple heroes should you really need help.
Q: What if I lose an item while being helped?
A: Cape Escape is not responsible for lost, stolen, or damaged property.
Q: How do I share my help request with someone else?
A: During your call for help you can share your progress with someone else using the Cape Escape app. Simply tap the “Share Help” button and select a contact. Soon, they’ll be able to watch the status of your hero.
Q: How do I rate my hero?
A: After you have received your help, you have the option to rate your hero on the app. Our heroes are customer obsessed and strive for 5 star ratings. Your feedback helps us improve our service!
Q: What if my hero doesn’t match the photo in the app?
A: The hero photo in your app should match the hero that arrives to pick you up. If they do not match, then Cape Escape recommends that you do not speak to that hero. You should then immediately report the imposter to Cape Escape.
Q: Can I use Concur with Cape Escape?
A: Yes, you can connect your Concur profile to the Cape Escape app so you can track business trips made on Cape Escape.
Q: Can I request a specific hero?
A: While we do not allow requesting specific heroes, you can specify the kind of help you need wile using the app.
Q: Why do you charge a service fee?
A: The service fee is a fixed charge added to every user. This helps us pay for our on-going maintenance and operating costs required to run the service and tend to our cohort of heroes.
